Mezinárodní Den Asexuality (MDA)

Goals

We want to support the connection of worldwide ace communities, share information and friendships, help people under the ace umbrella to not be ashamed or worried of their asexuality and rather celebrate it as an important part of themselves.

We hope to spread more information about asexuality to places where non-ace people can see it and learn more about it, so we can break the cycle of myths and false claims being spread. We also wish to make sure relevant information will reach ace people who may not be aware of their orientation yet.

Our aim is for asexual communities to grow as much as the public understanding and acceptance of our orientation(s).

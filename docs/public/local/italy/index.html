Giornata Internazionale dell’Asessualità (GIA)

Goal

Lettera A

We would like to use this day to celebrate the italian ace community. It is our impression that we use all the moments dedicated to asexuality to raise awareness about it, and while we think this objective is very important we also think it is as important to choose a moment dedicated only to us as ace people. Another important aspect to consider is the international charachter of this day, in which we’d like to talk about the worldwide asexual community, maybe working together with some other ace organization of another country. And lastly we would like to give an intersectional meaning to this day by reflecting on the place that the A has within the whole LGBTQIA community.

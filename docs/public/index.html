International Asexuality Day is on April 6th. IAD is for all people under the ace umbrella, including demisexual, grey-asexual and other identities.

The four themes of IAD are: Advocacy, Celebration, Education and Solidarity. These themes highlight the aims of recognising and raising awareness of the work that Ace advocacy organisations do, particularly in those countries that are not English-speaking and/or not western.

Participation in IAD is for anyone who identifies under the ace umbrella, and could be as simple as sharing a post on social media, but there also organisations all over the world who are holding events, running campaigns and supporting causes. You can look for your local organisation on our Find Local Advocates page.
